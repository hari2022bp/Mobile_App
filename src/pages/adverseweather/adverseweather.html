Date Time MIAL Safety Officer Name
Item For Inspection Yes No Remarks
1.Are the landing gears / nose wheel of all aircrafts choked securely?
2.Are the cabin and hold doors closed and locked properly?
3.Are the aircraft engine / pitot tuber covers properly secured and without any damage / wear and tear? Worn-out covers may become FOD.
4.Are the equipment usually in contact with the aircraft moved out of ERA? For e.g., step-ladders, BFLs, catering trucks, container dollies, trolleys etc.
5.Are all equipment / vehicles parked properly in ESA?
6.Are the parking brakes set or chocked on all parked GSE / Vehicles?
7.Are the wheel chokes / stabilizers deployed for GSE wherever parked?
8.Are the equipment with an elevated / lifting platform fully lowered and stabilizers deployed?
9.Are the stabilizers of maintenance steps fully engaged?
10.Are the containers securely closed and locked on the dollies properly?
11.Are empty ULDs properly secured?
12.Are any loose materials lying inside nearby / underneath the equipment / containers / dollies? Loose materials, if available, shall be cleared immediately as it may become FOD.
13.Is there any loose items such as cleaning tools, bins etc. kept unsecured?
14.Are FOD bins and other containers properly secured?
15.Are aprons in your jurisdiction clear of small loose items?
16.Are unsecured items in construction/ maintenance sites properly secured?
17.Whether the movable barricades placed at airside to secure the worksite and other areas are properly secured?
18.Whether the passenger boarding bridges(PBB) have been moved to its home/parking position? (When wind speed >=37 kts)
